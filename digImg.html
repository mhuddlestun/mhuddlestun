Arcane Majicks:

Arcane Majicks, 2022, text and image glitched triptych

This is a collection of glitched fantasy images intended to equate the ancient concepts regarding arcane magic and spells to digital functionality and visual production. The use of both text (notepad++) and audio (Audacity) programs to create these pieces marries these ancient concepts of spell casting and manipulation with current technologies.
